ABOUT PITHORAGARH

Pithoragarh, located in the easternmost part of the Indian state of Uttarakhand, is a picturesque district nestled in the lap of the Himalayas. Situated at an altitude ranging from 1,514 meters (4,967 feet) to 6,334 meters (20,781 feet) above sea level, Pithoragarh is known for its stunning natural beauty, diverse flora and fauna, and rich cultural heritage. The region boasts mesmerizing landscapes, snow-capped peaks, lush green valleys, and gushing rivers, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers, adventure enthusiasts, and spiritual seekers alike. Pithoragarh is home to several scenic spots and tourist attractions, including the Pithoragarh Fort, Kapileshwar Mahadev Temple, Chandak Hill, and Askot Wildlife Sanctuary. Visitors to Pithoragarh can indulge in activities such as trekking, mountaineering, camping, birdwatching, and exploring the vibrant local culture. With its serene ambiance and breathtaking vistas, Pithoragarh offers a rejuvenating escape from the hustle and bustle of city life.
